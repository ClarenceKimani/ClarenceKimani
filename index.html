Maasai Mara

The Maasai Mara is a renowned wildlife reserve located in southwestern Kenya, part of the larger Mara-Serengeti ecosystem. It is named after the Maasai people, the traditional inhabitants of the area, and the Mara River that runs through it. The Maasai Mara is globally recognized for its exceptional biodiversity, dramatic landscapes, and annual wildebeest migration, often described as one of the most spectacular natural events on Earth. Enjoy the tranquility and serene nature of the Maasai Mara. Enjoy the magnificent nature of the migration of Wildbeest here.


Mombasa

Mombasa, a coastal city in Kenya, is a vibrant hub of culture, history, and commerce. Situated on the shores of the Indian Ocean, it boasts a rich blend of Swahili, Arab, and European influences. With its strategic location, Mombasa has long been a key trading port, connecting East Africa with the wider world.


Lake Nakuru

On the floor of the Great Rift Valley, surrounded by wooded and bushy grassland, lies the beautiful and scenic Lake Nakuru National Park. Visitors can enjoy the wide ecological diversity and varied habitats that range from Lake Nakuru itself to the surrounding escarpment and picturesque ridges. Lake Nakuru National Park is ideal for bird watching, hiking,picnic and game drives.
